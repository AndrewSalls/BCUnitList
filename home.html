Welcome to

The (Unofficial) Battle Cats Collectopedia (BCC)!

Also known as BCUnitList

Navigation

  • Home

    • View this explanation, or credits (on the right).

  • View All Units

    • Displays all units at once, sorted by rarity.
    • Good for learning about units or changing a large number at once.

  • View Specific Unit

    • Displays a single unit, both their upgrades and evolution costs.
    • Good for quickly finding/modifying a unit.

  • Search by Category

    • Displays units by category.
    • Categories are units grouped by similarity, e.g. all being obtained from a certain gacha banner.

  • View Upgrade Costs

    • Based on upgrades given to units, shows the resources needed to maximally upgrade the unit.
    • XP, NP, evolution materials, etc.

  • Settings

    • Various settings for ease of use, managing save data, etc.
    • All save data is local to your browser. Make sure you back up your data before clearing cookies or local data!

  • Version Number

    • This website is updated manually.
    • If you can't find a unit or a unit is missing upgrades, it's probably because the website's data hasn't caught up to the game yet.
